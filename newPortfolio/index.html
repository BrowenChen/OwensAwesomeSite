Music

I have always been a lifelong musician, and currently am working towards solidifying my bebop jazz and harmony knowledge alongside my electronic music production techniques. Violin was introduced to me at the age of 5, where I went on to participate in local symphonies, school ensembles, jazz bands, drumlines, and rock bands. Currently, I play in UC Berkeley's Dann Zinn Adv Jazz combo, Big Band, and Nu Jazz Collective, alongside my fusion funk group Constant Time

Jazz-Fusion

I'm heavily interested in modern fusion - a genre that combines jazz harmony and theory with modern rock and pop elements

Electronic Production

I aim to create more EDM, Progressive House, Pop type tracks, eventually incorporating more fusion guitar and guitar synth ideas.
