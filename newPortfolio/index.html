Music

I have always been a lifelong musician, and at the moment am working towards solidifying my bebop jazz and harmony knowledge on guitar with my electronic music production and sound design techniques. I dabble with MIDI guitars as well. I currently play in UC Berkeley's Dann Zinn Advanced Jazz Combo, Big Band, and Nu Jazz Collective, alongside my fusion funk group Constant Time

Jazz-Fusion

I'm heavily interested in modern fusion - a genre that combines jazz harmony and theory with modern rock and pop elements. Interested in the neuroscience of jazz improvisation?

Electronic Production

I aim to create more EDM, Progressive House, Pop type tracks, eventually incorporating more fusion guitar and guitar synth ideas. Currently use Logic Pro X, Omnisphere, Serum, and Massive for production and sound design.
